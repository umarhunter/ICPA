It all started in college

Learning computer science principles in college is a great tool for being able to improve your programming abilities. However, a group of college students began to wonder, how can a programmer get better outside of school?

Easy enough answer, right? Keep writing code! You will definitely improve your skills that way.

The members of this group agreed, but there was something more important to them than just improving their programming skills alone.

They wanted to learn and create together as a group and see what they can do. One programmer is capable of many tasks, a group, much more.

This is how the idea of ICPA was formed.
